# Pet Loving Simulator

A cute browser game where you choose a pet (bunny, dog, cat, or hamster), name them, and take care of them with interactive mini-games.

## How to play

1. **Choose a pet** – Bunny, Dog, Cat, or Hamster  
2. **Name your pet**  
3. **Take care of them:**
   - **Feed** – Pick food or drinks and drag them to your pet’s mouth (mouse or finger)
   - **Play** – Choose a toy and watch your pet run around and play
   - **Clean** – Go to the shower room and drag the soap over your pet to clean them
   - **Coddle** – Stroke or tap your pet with your finger or mouse to make them happy
   - **Visit** & **Care** – Quick check-in and general care

Works on desktop (mouse) and mobile (touch). Just open `index.html` in your browser.

## Tech

Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript – no build step, no frameworks.
